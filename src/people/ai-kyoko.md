Ai graduated from Soai College. She was discovered by a talent scout while
working at a coffee shop and began her career appearing in numerous television
series during the 1960s.

Her most famous role came in 1968 when she played the Kilaak Queen in the Toho
_kaijû_ film _Destroy All Monsters_. As the leader of the alien Kilaaks, she
commanded the Earth's monsters to attack major cities. She also appeared in the
film _Kigeki ekimae gakuen_ (1967) and provided a voice for the Toei animated
film _Flying Phantom Ship_ (1969).
