Ai graduated from Soai College. She was discovered by a talent scout while working at a coffee shop and began her career appearing in numerous television series during the 1960s.

Her most famous role came in 1968 when she played the Kilaak Queen in the Toho *kaijû* film *Destroy All Monsters*. As the leader of the alien Kilaaks, she commanded the Earth's monsters to attack major cities. She also appeared in the film *Kigeki ekimae gakuen* (1967) and provided a voice for the Toei animated film *Flying Phantom Ship* (1969).
